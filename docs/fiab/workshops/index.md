# CSA Loom — 5-Day Cloud CoE Workshops

CSA Loom ships **two 5-day Cloud Center of Excellence workshops**
day-one (per AMENDMENTS §A12 / OQ-13):

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-shield-account: [**5-Day Federal CoE Workshop**](5-day-federal-coe/index.md)

    For federal civilian agencies, DoD components, intelligence
    community, state + local government, federal contractors (CMMC
    L2/L3, ITAR). FedRAMP / IL4 / IL5 emphasis.

-   :material-domain: [**5-Day Commercial CoE Workshop**](5-day-commercial-coe/index.md)

    For regulated commercial verticals waiting for Fabric Gov —
    healthcare (HIPAA), regional banks (sovereignty), pharma (FDA
    Part 11), defense industrial base. Commercial Azure boundary.

</div>

## Common structure

Both workshops follow the same 5-day shape:

| Day | Focus |
|---|---|
| 1 | Foundation & Deploy — kickoff, install, first DLZ |
| 2 | Ingest & Mirroring & Catalog — onboard sources |
| 3 | Transform & Lakehouse & Warehouse — medallion patterns |
| 4 | BI & AI & Direct Lake & Data Agents — Power BI, semantic models, NL agents |
| 5 | Operate & Govern & Forward-Migrate — Day-2 ops + forward plan |

## Outcomes (both)

By end of day 5:
- A working CSA Loom Admin Plane + 1 DLZ in customer's Azure
  subscription
- One real workload migrated / piloted on Loom
- An Activator rule firing into Teams
- A Data Agent answering questions
- Documented CoE charter (governance, naming, RBAC, capacity,
  costs, operations)
- Forward-migration plan to Fabric
- Trained customer team capable of operating Loom independently

## Workshop materials shipped

- Day-by-day facilitator guides
- Hands-on lab guides for participants
- Sample datasets (CUI-safe synthetic)
- Slide decks per day (auto-generated from markdown)
- Pre-workshop readiness checklist
- Post-workshop satisfaction survey + outcome metrics
- CoE charter template

## Workshop format

- 4-8 platform engineers (primary participants)
- + Extended stakeholders for kickoff + day-5 readout (architect,
  security, BI lead, exec sponsor)
- On-site at customer OR virtual classroom
- Each day: 9am-5pm with 1-hr lunch + 2x 15-min breaks
