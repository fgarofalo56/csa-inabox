## Why this is short

Most platform thinking lives in **ADRs** (one decision per file, focused) and **Best Practices** (operational, not theoretical). The Research section is reserved for cross-cutting analysis that doesn't fit either format — typically annual platform direction reviews and competitive landscape work.

If you have a research question we haven't published on, open a discussion at https://github.com/fgarofalo56/csa-inabox/discussions.
