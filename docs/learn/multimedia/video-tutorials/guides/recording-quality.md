# Recording Quality Guide

Best practices for high-quality video recording.

## 📋 Overview

Ensure your video tutorials maintain professional quality standards.

## 🎥 Recording Tips

- Use proper lighting
- Choose quiet environment
- Test audio levels
- Check framing and composition

## 💡 Best Practices

- Record in high resolution
- Use external microphone
- Maintain consistent quality
- Follow brand guidelines
